Mentoria
Overview
Mentoria is a subscription-based digital platform designed to support athletes and young professionals in navigating their careers both during and after sport. The platform combines education, advisory services, and AI-driven tools to provide structured, long-term career management.
Mentoria addresses a critical gap in the market: the lack of holistic career support for athletes beyond performance on the field.
Vision
To become the leading global platform for athlete career management, bridging sport, education, and long-term wealth creation.
Mission
To empower athletes with the tools, knowledge, and network required to build sustainable and successful careers beyond sport.
Problem
Athletes often lack structured career planning
Limited access to trusted legal, financial, and educational advisory
Poor transition support after retirement
Fragmented services across agents, advisors, and educators
Solution
Mentoria provides an integrated ecosystem including:
Centralized knowledge database
Career development programs
Legal and financial advisory access
Sponsorship and brand management support
Post-career transition planning
AI-powered personalized recommendations (future phase)
